Styling & Theming

Core Features

1

Authentication & Roles

Secure JWT-based authentication. Features distinct dashboards and permissions for Students, Instructors, and Admins.

2

Course & Video Management

Instructors can create courses, upload thumbnails via Cloudinary, and add categorized video lessons with detailed descriptions.

3

Razorpay Payment Gateway

Integrated INR (₹) payments using Razorpay API for seamless course purchases with instant access validation.

4

Progress & Certification

Tracks video completion percentage. Automatically generates a downloadable PDF Certificate when progress reaches 100%.

5

Quizzes & Assignments

Domain-specific multiple-choice quizzes are available inside the course player, offering instant grading and success feedback.

6

Live Q&A Forum

Interactive discussion forum within each course. Students can ask questions, reply to peers, upvote, and receive answers from Instructors.

System Workflow

❖

1. Registration & Access

Users register. Instructors create courses in Draft mode, publish them, and they become visible on the public Storefront.

❖

2. Enrollment & Transaction

Student clicks "Access Course", completes the Razorpay transaction, and the Backend securely creates an `Enrollment` record linking the user to the course.

❖

3. Learning Experience

Student accesses the Course Preview Modal. They watch videos (marking as complete), take Quizzes for grading, and participate in the Q&A Forum.

❖

4. Achievement

Upon 100% video completion, the backend updates progress, allowing the user to generate and download their Completion Certificate from their Dashboard.
